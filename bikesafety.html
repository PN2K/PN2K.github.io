Bike Safety!

PN2K is working hard to make our communities a safer place for cyclists, starting with the MIT/Cambridge/Boston communities!

Upcoming Bike Safety Events

MIT PN2K - GSC Sustainability Bike Fest 2014: Sunday, September 7th

MIT PN2K and GSC Sustainability will be partnering with MIT PTO, Hubway, Whole Foods, and WMBR to throw a festival for MIT bike riders, both new and experienced! See www.PN2K.org/MIT/bikefest.html for more details and to register for the event.

Recent Bike Safety Events

Free Bike Light Giveaway with the Christina Clarke Genco Foundation: Thursday, May 29th

The PN2K and CCG Foundations joined forces to install free bike lights on bikes for cyclists riding past without lights. Look at all these awesome smiling, happy faces, keeping safe on the road :D We encourage all cyclists to use bike lights when cycling in the dark. A light not only helps you see, it also helps drivers nearby to know you are there. Thank you CCG for providing the bike lights and thank you to your awesome team for the installations.

Still in need of a bike light? Be on the look out for future bike light handouts to come.

2014 MIT Bike Awareness Day with the MIT Parking and Transportation Office: Wednesday, May 14th

PN2K partnered with the MIT Parking & Transportation Office on Bike Awareness Day at MIT. There were free T-shirts, bike lights, and basic repairs, courtesy of Cambridge Bicycle, and snacks at the PN2K booth, courtesy of Shaw's Supermarket! Students also had the opportunity to register their bikes and get tips on safer cycling. Thank you for all who joined in our festivities! Photos from the day’s event can be found here

2014 MassCommute Bicycle Challenge: May 10th-May 18th

Thank you to all our volunteers who joined us in this year’s MassCommute Bicycle Challenge! See you all again next year!
