We're a non-profit organization focused on education.

Education in Bike Safety

PN2K works to make cycling safer for everyone, starting at MIT, Cambridge, and Boston. MIT PN2K (our MIT-funded and ASA-approved student group at MIT) exists to work primarily on bike safety for the MIT community.

Education in Academia

Currently our largest partner is MIT Amphibious Achievement. Our work in this area involves offering more opportunities to equal access education for underprivileged youth.

Bike Safety Workshops

PN2K is working to implement a 2-part bike safety event at MIT, which will take place twice during the year - once in the Fall and the other during the Spring.

Bike Light Giveaways

PN2K and CCG Foundations join forces to install free bike lights on bikes for cyclists riding past without lights.

Academic Events with MIT Amphibious Achievement

PN2K organizes exciting on-site visits to local companies, start-ups, and nonprofits for Achievers each semester to inspire them to pursue higher education and introduce them to potential career paths.
