Join Our Team!

Open Volunteer Positions and Committees for PN2K:

  • Co-President


  • Treasurer


  • Secretary


  • Webmaster


  • Co-Social Chair
    • Goal of position: Organize socials for PN2K members and cyclists.

  • Co-Bike Safety Chair
    • Goal of position: Organize monthly bike events & rides.

  • Co-Publicity Chair
    • Goal of position: Spread the world thorugh marketing and social media.

  • Co-Historian
    • Goal of position: Keep records of accomplishments, activities, photos, videos and updates the blog.

  • Bike Safety Committee
    • Goal of Committee: Organizes and runs operations of bike events & rides.

  • Street Safety Committee
    • Goal of Committee: Work to make the MIT Sailing Pavilion crosswalks and Mass Ave & Vassar St intersection safer.

  • Product Design Committee
    • Goal of Committee: Design products and oversee distribution of products.

  • Community Ambassador Groups (Public Relations Agents)
    • Goal of position: for those who want to test out and refine their people skills - ideate on where and how PN2K can add value to our existing partnerships; serve as point persons/liaisons for existing partnerships (e.g., MIT Amphibz, MIT bike safety programs, Danger!Awesome, EcoVent, etc.); recruit volunteers in local communities to join our initiatives.

  • Marketing/Promotion (Idea Generators)
    Goal of position: for those who like design and want to have their try at marketing the PN2K brand to the public and organisations/companies to increase visibility; develop promotional materials and new product lines; maintain social media updates; create newsletters, pamphlets, promotional videos, and other materials that are used when fundraising and as updates to donors.


  • Fundraising (Money Masters)
    Goal of position: for the creative minds who know how to generate funds for project collaborations; ideate on local cafés and donors to reach out to; search for grants and write grant proposals; for those who wants to be active in attending events where we can give out wristbands and show appreciation to our supportive donors.


  • Technical Group/IT Support Team
    Goal of position: Pretty self-explanatory. Like IT stuff? This would be the position for you!


  • Other/General Volunteer
    None of these descriptions quite fit what you are looking for? Email us and we can work together to create the perfect experience for you.
